About

I received my Ph.D. in Management (Strategy) from Purdue University, M.Sc. in Business and Technology Management from KAIST, and B.A. in Economics from Sungkyunkwan University.

My research examines how firms and employees respond to major corporate events—including completed and terminated M&A deals, as well as bankruptcy. On the firm side, I study subsequent corporate development and strategic hiring; on the employee side, I investigate career mobility and entrepreneurship decisions. To address these questions, I use large-scale datasets, natural language processing, and econometric methods.
