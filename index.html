About

My research explores how major corporate events—such as M&A, deal terminations, and bankruptcy—reshape firm strategy and individual careers. I examine how firms pursue subsequent corporate development and strategic hiring, and how employees make decisions about mobility and entrepreneurship.

Originally from South Korea, I received my Ph.D. in Management (Strategy Area) from Purdue University, M.Sc. in Business and Technology Management from KAIST, and B.A. in Economics from Sungkyunkwan University. Prior to academia, I worked at Samsung Electronics and PwC.
