About

I am an Assistant Professor of Strategic Management at Kennesaw State University. I received my Ph.D. in Management (Strategy) from Purdue University, M.Sc. in Business and Technology Management from KAIST, and B.A. in Economics from Sungkyunkwan University.

My research interests include strategic human capital, entrepreneurship, and corporate strategy. My work has been recognized with the Academy of Management STR Division Distinguished Paper Award and SMS Best Paper Honorable Mention.
