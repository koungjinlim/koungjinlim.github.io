About

My research lies at the intersection of strategic human capital, entrepreneurship, and corporate strategy. I explore how major corporate events—such as mergers and acquisitions, deal terminations, and bankruptcy—reshape firm strategy and individual careers.

Originally from South Korea, I received my Ph.D. in Management (Strategy Area) from Purdue University, M.Sc. in Business and Technology Management from KAIST, and B.A. in Economics from Sungkyunkwan University. Prior to academia, I worked at Samsung Electronics and PwC.
