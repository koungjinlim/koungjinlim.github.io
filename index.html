About

I am an Assistant Professor of Strategic Management at the Michael A. Leven School, Coles College of Business. I received my Ph.D. from Purdue University, M.Sc. from KAIST, and B.A. from Sungkyunkwan University.

My research lies at the intersection of strategic human capital, entrepreneurship, and corporate strategy.
